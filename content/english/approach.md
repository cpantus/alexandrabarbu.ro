method_tabs:

      slug: "cbt"
      icon: "brain"
      description: |
        CBT is a structured, solution-oriented method that helps you identify and modify thoughts and behaviors
        that contribute to your difficulties. It is effective for anxiety, depression, panic attacks, and many other challenges.
      benefits:
        - "Demonstrable results in 12-20 sessions"
        - "Practical tools you can use daily"
        - "Focus on the present and current problem-solving"
        - "Scientifically proven effectiveness for many conditions"
    - name: "Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT)"
      slug: "eft"
      icon: "heart"
      description: |
        EFT helps you understand and process your deep emotions, often rooted in early attachment relationships.
        This method is particularly useful for relationship difficulties, attachment issues, and healing emotional trauma.
      benefits:
        - "Deep processing of emotions and needs"
        - "Improving intimate relationships and secure attachment"
        - "Emotional healing and increased self-awareness"
        - "Effective for couples and individual therapy"
    - name: "Solution-Focused Therapy (SFT)"
      slug: "sft"
      icon: "lightbulb"
      description: |
        SFT emphasizes your resources, your strengths, and the future you want, rather than past problems.
        It is a pragmatic approach, focused on concrete goals and rapid change.
      benefits:
        - "Focus on solutions, not problems"
        - "Rapid results (often in 5-8 sessions)"
        - "Using your resources and strengths"
        - "Ideal for clear and specific goals"
    - name: "Mindfulness and ACT"
      slug: "mindfulness-act"
      icon: "spa"
      description: |
        Mindfulness and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) teach you to be present in the current moment,
        to accept difficult experiences, and to act in accordance with your values. These methods are excellent
        for reducing stress, anxiety, and increasing overall well-being.
      benefits:
        - "Reducing stress and anxiety"
        - "Increasing self-awareness and presence"
        - "Accepting difficult experiences without avoidance"
        - "Clarifying values and value-aligned action"
