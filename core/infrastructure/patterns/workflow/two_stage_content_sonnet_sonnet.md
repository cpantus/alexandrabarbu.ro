# Pattern: two_stage_content_sonnet_sonnet

**Category**: workflow | **Complexity**: complex | **Thinking**: think-hard
**Knowledge Required**: brand-voice-guidelines (full), audience-research (full), campaign-strategy-frameworks
**Quality Mode**: Maximum strategic depth for critical work

---

## PURPOSE

Generate brand-defining content with maximum quality through deep strategic exploration and systematic review.

**Key innovation:** Sonnet generates with deep thinking ($0.08), Sonnet reviews with strategic analysis ($0.04) = $0.12 total = **Same cost as direct sonnet work, but structured quality process ensures best-of-best thinking**.

---

## INPUT

**Required:**
- TASK: High-stakes content generation (brand manifestos, positioning statements, major campaign headlines, strategic narratives)
- QUANTITY: Number of variants to generate (typically 5-10 for deep exploration)
- TARGET: Persona (Strategic Sarah / Technical Tom) or audience description
- STAKES: Description of why this is critical (budget, visibility, brand impact)

**Optional:**
- GOAL: Awareness / Engagement / Conversion
- PLATFORM: LinkedIn / Email / Blog / Landing Page / Campaign
- CONSTRAINTS: Length limits, keyword requirements, messaging guardrails
- BUDGET: Campaign budget or visibility estimate (helps calibrate quality bar)

---

## PROCESS

### Stage 1: Deep Strategic Generation (Sonnet with think-hard)

**Use copywriter subagent with extended thinking:**

```
"Use copywriter subagent to generate [QUANTITY] [content type] variants for [TASK].

This is CRITICAL WORK:
[Explain why: $X budget, brand-defining, executive visibility, etc.]

Target: [PERSONA]
Goal: [GOAL]
Platform: [PLATFORM]
Constraints: [specific requirements]

Instructions:
1. **THINK HARD** about strategic approach first:
   - What makes this moment unique for the brand?
   - What is the core insight or truth we're revealing?
   - How do we differentiate from category norms?
   - What emotional and rational appeals do we need?
   - What is the strategic narrative arc?

2. Generate [QUANTITY] variants, each exploring a distinct strategic angle:
   - Benefit-focused (solve pain point, show transformation)
   - Insight-driven (reveal non-obvious truth)
   - Aspiration-led (paint vision of future state)
   - Provocation (challenge status quo)
   - Story-based (narrative that resonates emotionally)
   - Data-anchored (lead with compelling proof)

3. For each variant, provide:
   - The variant itself
   - Strategic rationale (why this angle, what makes it powerful)
   - Expected emotional response from target persona
   - Brand positioning reinforcement (how it strengthens brand)

4. Load FULL brand voice guidelines (not compressed)
   - Review voice-guidelines.md, tone-by-format.md, vocabulary-guide.md
   - Check forbidden terms and compliance requirements
   - Verify messaging hierarchy alignment

5. Load FULL persona research
   - Deep pain points, aspirations, language patterns
   - Decision-making triggers and objection handlers
   - Content preferences and engagement patterns

6. Include your top 3 picks with detailed strategic reasoning

7. Format output with clear strategic thinking documented"
```

**Expected output from Sonnet (Generator):**
- 5-10 deeply considered variants
- Strategic rationale for each
- Emotional/positioning analysis
- Top 3 recommendations with reasoning
- Total cost: ~$0.08
- Time: 3-5 minutes

- Detailed scoring matrix for all variants
- Top 3 selections with comprehensive analysis
- Risk assessment for each
- A/B testing strategy

- Total cost: ~$0.04
- Time: 2-3 minutes
